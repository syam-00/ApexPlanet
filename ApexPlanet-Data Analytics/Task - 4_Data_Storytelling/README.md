## Hypothesis Testing

### Business Hypothesis

Technology products generate higher average profit compared to Furniture products.

### Null Hypothesis (H0)

There is no significant difference in average profit between Technology and Furniture categories.

### Alternative Hypothesis (H1)

Technology category has higher average profit than Furniture.

### Statistical Test Used

Two-sample T-Test

### Interpretation

If the p-value is less than 0.05, the null hypothesis is rejected and the result is statistically significant.

This indicates that Technology products generate significantly higher profit.
